My Neighborhood Tour

Scalino

Scalino is an extremely tasty Italian restarant in Greenpoint BK that is authentic, and somewhat cheap.

Best Dishes

  1. Cacio e pepe
  2. Gorgonzola Gnocchi
  3. Classic Bolognese

G train

The G train is used by many residents of Brooklyn.
The G runs down Brooklyn,making Brooklyn transportation easier.


Mcarren Park

Mcarren park is a popular park in Greenpoint that has lots of grass to play sports on, which is what people do
There are many soccer clubs that practice and play there, school football and track teams practice there as well.
